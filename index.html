juxtaphi.com

A field guide for choosing how to live — by setting two philosophies side by side.

Each issue takes two schools of thought and compares them across the seven domains of life: failure, habits, meaning, death, ambition, relationships, and emotions. The goal is not to pick a winner. The goal is to help you see what each tradition asks of you, and what it gives back.
